ABOUT ME

I'm Emerson Sanchez, a digital artist exploring all fields of design. 


I have around three years of experience in graphic design, layout design, and 3D design including my current experiences at the Global Business and Digital Arts program at the University of Waterloo. The program strongly emphasizes UI/UX design, leaving only a bit of space for other design fields. However, this restriction did not stop me from specializing in various design fields. With strong collaboration skills and adaptability, I can design in any environment bringing new solutions to any challenge.


In my free time, you can find me glued to my computer finding new design techniques and experimenting with creative ideas.
